My name is Nick.

I write software

and graffiti

I like writing code, experimenting with spray-paint and listening to A Tribe Called Quest. I grew up in Portland, OR raised on video games, hip-hop and basketball. To be specific I grew up on GoldenEye, The Legend of Zelda, Jay-Z and NaS. I graduated from Oregon State University cum laude in computer science with a minor in business entrepreneurship. I currently live in Seattle, WA where I work as a developer for Artefact. I'm passionately curious about technology, art, and design. When I'm not messing around with Objective-C, JavaScript, Node.js, or Ruby I'm probably scribbling on a canvas listening to De La Soul.
